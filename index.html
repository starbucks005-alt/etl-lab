Mission Briefing

Dr. Terry L. Oroszi is a transformational leader with 30 years of cross-sector innovation spanning academia, military, government, and nonprofit environments. As Director of the Emerging Technologies Laboratory and Vice Chair of Pharmacology & Toxicology at Wright State University's Boonshoft School of Medicine, she is at the forefront of integrating AI and emerging technologies into health sciences education and biodefense research.

ETL Teaching Framework

Systematic Literature Review Workshop

A structured, data-driven SLR methodology taught to graduate students and clinicians — designed to compress months of work into a publishable manuscript

THE METHOD
Gold-Standard SLR
Accelerated

A strategic workflow integrating the scientific rigor of the Systematic Literature Review (SLR) with data-driven tools to accelerate the process from keyword search to publishable manuscript — without sacrificing rigor or reproducibility.

Taught as a Grand Rounds workshop to graduate students, PharmD candidates, and physician researchers at BSOM. Uses live data from PubMed, pivot tables, and mind maps to teach argument structure, research gap identification, and journal targeting in a single session.
